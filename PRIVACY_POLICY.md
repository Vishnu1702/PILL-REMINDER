# Privacy Policy for MyMedAlert - Medicine Reminder

**Last Updated: December 2, 2025**

## Introduction

MyMedAlert ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Medicine Information**: Names, dosages, and schedules of medications you add to the app
- **Patient Names**: Names you optionally provide for tracking medicines for different people
- **Emergency Contacts**: Phone numbers you optionally add for emergency calling features

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Device Information**: We may collect device identifiers for app functionality
- **Usage Data**: How you interact with the app to improve user experience

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Schedule and deliver medicine reminder notifications
- Display your medicine schedules and history
- Enable emergency contact calling features
- Improve app functionality and user experience

## Data Storage

**All your data is stored locally on your device only.** We do not:
- Upload your health data to any servers
- Share your information with third parties
- Sell your personal information

Your medicine schedules, dosage history, and personal information never leave your device unless you explicitly share them.

## Permissions We Request

Our app requests the following permissions:

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| Notifications | To send medicine reminders |
| Alarms | To schedule exact-time reminders |
| Vibration | To alert you with vibration |
| Wake Lock | To ensure alarms work when phone is sleeping |
| Boot Completed | To reschedule alarms after device restart |
| Phone (optional) | To enable emergency contact calling |

## Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:
- All data is stored locally on your device
- No internet transmission of personal health data
- No cloud storage of your medicine information

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data (stored locally on your device)
- Delete your data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data
- Control notification permissions through device settings
